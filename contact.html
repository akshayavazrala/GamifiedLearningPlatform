Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register my students?

Teachers can register students through the teacher dashboard by clicking on "Add Students" and following the simple process.

Is the platform available in Odia language?

Yes, our platform supports both English and Odia languages. You can switch between languages in your profile settings.

What devices do students need?

Students can access our platform on any device with a web browser - smartphones, tablets, laptops, or desktop computers.

How can I track student progress?

Teachers have access to a comprehensive dashboard that shows individual and class progress across all subjects and lessons.

Are the lessons aligned with state curriculum?

Yes, all our lessons are designed to align with the Odisha state curriculum for Class 6 students.

How do I reset my password?

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the instructions sent to your registered email address.
