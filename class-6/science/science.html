Why Science is Important for Class 6 Students

Science education at the Class 6 level plays a crucial role in developing young minds. It helps students understand the world around them, fosters curiosity, and builds a foundation for future scientific learning.

Through hands-on experiments and interactive learning, students develop critical thinking skills, learn to ask questions, and discover how to find answers through observation and experimentation.

Our curriculum is designed specifically for Odisha students, incorporating local examples and contexts to make learning more relevant and engaging.

Develops Curiosity

Encourages students to ask questions about natural phenomena

Builds Problem-Solving Skills

Teaches systematic approaches to understanding and solving problems

Promotes Environmental Awareness

Helps students understand and appreciate their natural environment

Prepares for Future Learning

Builds a strong foundation for advanced science concepts

What You'll Learn in Class 6 Science

Food & Nutrition

  • Sources of food from plants and animals
  • Components of food and their functions
  • Balanced diet and its importance
  • Deficiency diseases and prevention

Materials & Fabrics

  • Classification of materials
  • Natural and synthetic fibres
  • Process of making fabric from fibre
  • Properties of different materials

Plants & Animals

  • Parts of plants and their functions
  • Process of photosynthesis
  • Habitat and adaptation
  • Characteristics of living organisms

Water & Environment

  • Importance of water for life
  • Water cycle and conservation
  • Air and its composition
  • Waste management and recycling

Motion & Measurement

  • Types of motion
  • Measurement of distance and time
  • Standard units of measurement
  • Simple measuring instruments

Electricity & Circuits

  • Electric current and circuits
  • Conductors and insulators
  • Simple electric devices
  • Safety with electricity
