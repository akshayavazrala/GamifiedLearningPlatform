Food: Where Does It Come From?

Food is essential for all living organisms. It provides energy for various activities, helps in growth, and repairs damaged body parts. Let's explore where our food comes from and how it reaches our plate.

Sources of Food

  • Plants are the primary source of food
  • Animals provide us with products like milk, eggs, and meat
  • Food can come from both land and water sources
  • Different parts of plants are used as food

Types of Food

  • Carbohydrates: Provide energy (e.g., rice, wheat)
  • Proteins: Help in growth and repair (e.g., pulses, eggs)
  • Fats: Store energy (e.g., oils, butter)
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Protect from diseases

Food Chain

  • Plants are producers - they make their own food
  • Animals that eat plants are herbivores
  • Animals that eat other animals are carnivores
  • Animals that eat both plants and animals are omnivores
